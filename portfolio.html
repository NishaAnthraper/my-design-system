I lead UX and product strategy at Bristol Myers Squibb, working across research, design, product ownership, and the operational layer that holds it all together — the part most people don't see but everyone feels when it's missing.

A decade in, I've learned that the most important work happens before anyone opens a design tool — reframing the problem, getting stakeholders to the same truth, and making sure the team is solving the right thing. That's where I focus.

I'm a generalist by nature. I've built products from 0→1 and scaled them past that. I share openly, and know from experience that the gap between a good idea and a shipped product is almost entirely about the people.
